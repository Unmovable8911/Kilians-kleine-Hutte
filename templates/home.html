To Share my Idears and Interests

Welcome to my humble website! I’m Kilian, the author and curator of this site. You might be wondering what “Kilians kleine Hütte” means. It’s German for “Kilian’s little cabin”. I chose this name because this site is like a cozy and secluded cabin in the woods, where I keep all my treasures: my ideas, my passions, my favorites.

On this site, you will find various things that I love and want to share with you, such as music, movies, books, and more. I hope you will enjoy exploring my little cabin and discovering what makes me happy. Thank you for visiting!
